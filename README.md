## SEO — EcoVerify

As part of my placement year at **[EcoVerify](https://www.ecoverify.co.uk/)**, a UK-based sustainability consultancy, I took ownership of the company's SEO strategy and website optimisation.

**What I did:**

- Conducted in-depth keyword research using **Google Keyword Planner** and **Ahrefs** to identify high-value, low-competition terms in the sustainability and EcoVadis space
- Analysed competitor backlink profiles and developed a backlink acquisition strategy
- Optimised on-page SEO across the website — meta titles, descriptions, heading structure, internal linking, and content relevance
- Monitored rankings and adjusted strategy based on performance data

**Results:**

- EcoVerify now ranks on the **first page of Google** for target keywords
- The company appears in the **top 3 results on ChatGPT** — a strong indicator of GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) performance, meaning the brand is being referenced by AI tools when users ask relevant questions
- This dual presence across traditional search and AI-generated results reflects a forward-thinking SEO approach that goes beyond standard practice

## YouTube Optimisation

I have managed and optimised YouTube channels on behalf of clients, focusing on maximising visibility and monetisation potential through best-practice SEO.

**What I did:**

- Used **TubeBuddy** to analyse video SEO scores and identify optimisation opportunities across existing and new content
- Researched and applied high-performing, relevant tags to improve discoverability
- Optimised video titles and descriptions for keyword relevance and click-through rate
- Audited thumbnails for visual effectiveness and consistency with channel branding
- Checked and implemented **end screens** and **cards** in line with the latest YouTube best practice guidelines
- Monitored channel analytics via TubeBuddy dashboard — tracking impressions, click-through rate, average view duration, and subscriber growth
- Stayed up to date with YouTube algorithm updates to ensure all optimisations reflected current platform requirements
